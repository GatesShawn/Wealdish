Forms abstract nouns.
Territory controlled by, -ship, -age, -y, -dom.
## Usage notes
With names of leaders, [[-æd]] can be both concrete and abstract, for instance iarllaeth (“earldom”) is both the rank of being an earl and the territory controlled by one.
